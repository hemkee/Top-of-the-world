On Top Of The World

Most exhilarating experience!!!

Flying in the hot air balloon was one of the foremost entries of my bucket list. Most enjoyable moment. Got to see the beautiful landscape and green Panorama of Goa. Our India is really beautiful.

The day began in the early hours of the morning at 6:30am at the Assolda Football Grounds in Chandor. We watched the balloon being prepared for flight (which takes almost an hour) as we received a short safety briefing and the do’s and don’ts of hot air balloon travel. It's a mammoth task to get the enormous balloon into action and takes almost 10 people to control it. The pilot and crew are professionally trained and ensure that strict safety norms are followed. The total in-air time is approximately 1 hour. The route of the flight totally depends direction of the wind on that day. Flights are subject to favorable weather conditions, and there a chance of cancellation if weather does not permit.

We were awe inspired by our Pilot Mrs.Reetu Yadav. A fearless lady with a great sense of humour, she maneuvered the balloon with ease and precision. We flew for 45 minutes and landed 8 kms away in a field. It's a 360-degree Bird's eye view from a peak elevation of 2000ft was across the sun-kissed fields, delicate Mandovi river and bold mountains covered by silver lined clouds. The warmth from the morning sun is a joy beyond comparison. We also spotted an American Eagle flying majestically beneath us. We felt like small kids waving to the cheerful natives exchanging morning greetings !!
